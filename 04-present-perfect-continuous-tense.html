Present Perfect Continuous
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + have/has been + V-ing
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
⏳
Aksi mulai di lampau & masih berlangsung kini
Menggambarkan tindakan yang dimulai di masa lalu dan masih terus berjalan sampai sekarang.
"They have been waiting for the bus for twenty minutes."
🌿
Aktivitas baru-baru ini dengan hasil sekarang
Aktivitas yang berlangsung belakangan ini dan meninggalkan bukti atau hasil yang terlihat sekarang.
"Her eyes are red because she has been crying."
📏
Penekanan durasi (since/for)
Menegaskan berapa lama sebuah aktivitas sudah berlangsung dengan "since" atau "for".
"We have been living in Jakarta since 2015."
❓
Menanyakan berapa lama (how long)
Menanyakan durasi sebuah aktivitas yang masih berlangsung dengan "how long".
"How long have you been studying French?"
2
Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat
(+) Positif
S+have/has+been+V-ing
(−) Negatif
S+have/hasnotbeen+V-ing
(?) Tanya
Have/Has+S+been+V-ing?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ have/has been = auxiliary verb (has been → he/she/it; have been → I/you/we/they)  |  ■ V-ing = kata kerja + akhiran -ing (present participle)
3
Contoh Kalimat
Penggunaan nyata dalam kalimat
My brother has been repairing the old motorcycle all morning.Kakak laki-laki saya sudah memperbaiki motor tua itu sepanjang pagi. Aksi yang berlangsung terus-menerus sejak tadi pagi hingga sekarang
The children have been playing in the garden since lunchtime.Anak-anak sudah bermain di taman sejak waktu makan siang. Aksi dimulai di masa lalu dan masih berlangsung sampai kini
I have been reading this novel for three hours.Saya sudah membaca novel ini selama tiga jam. Penekanan pada durasi aktivitas dengan "for"
She has been teaching mathematics at the university lately.Dia akhir-akhir ini mengajar matematika di universitas. Aktivitas berkelanjutan baru-baru ini
Have they been painting the fence the whole week?Apakah mereka sudah mengecat pagar sepanjang minggu ini? Pertanyaan tentang aktivitas yang berlangsung selama suatu periode
4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ HAVE / HAS BEEN — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Aturan auxiliary: Gunakan has been untuk subjek tunggal orang ketiga (he, she, it), dan have been untuk I, you, we, they. Setelah "been", kata kerja selalu memakai akhiran -ing.
PronounTipeKalimat
I (+) I have been cooking dinner for the guests for an hour.Saya sudah memasak makan malam untuk para tamu selama satu jam.
(−) I have not been cooking dinner for the guests for an hour.Saya belum memasak makan malam untuk para tamu selama satu jam.
(?) Have I been cooking dinner for the guests for an hour?Apakah saya sudah memasak makan malam untuk para tamu selama satu jam?
He (+) He has been writing letters to his pen pal all evening.Dia sudah menulis surat untuk sahabat penanya sepanjang malam.
(−) He has not been writing letters to his pen pal all evening.Dia belum menulis surat untuk sahabat penanya sepanjang malam.
(?) Has he been writing letters to his pen pal all evening?Apakah dia sudah menulis surat untuk sahabat penanya sepanjang malam?
She (+) She has been practicing the piano since this morning.Dia sudah berlatih piano sejak pagi ini.
(−) She has not been practicing the piano since this morning.Dia belum berlatih piano sejak pagi ini.
(?) Has she been practicing the piano since this morning?Apakah dia sudah berlatih piano sejak pagi ini?
It (+) It has been raining heavily all afternoon.Hujan sudah turun dengan deras sepanjang sore.
(−) It has not been raining heavily all afternoon.Hujan belum turun dengan deras sepanjang sore.
(?) Has it been raining heavily all afternoon?Apakah hujan sudah turun dengan deras sepanjang sore?
You (+) You have been learning Spanish for two years.Kamu sudah belajar bahasa Spanyol selama dua tahun.
(−) You have not been learning Spanish for two years.Kamu belum belajar bahasa Spanyol selama dua tahun.
(?) Have you been learning Spanish for two years?Apakah kamu sudah belajar bahasa Spanyol selama dua tahun?
We (+) We have been building a treehouse in the backyard since dawn.Kami sudah membangun rumah pohon di halaman belakang sejak fajar.
(−) We have not been building a treehouse in the backyard since dawn.Kami belum membangun rumah pohon di halaman belakang sejak fajar.
(?) Have we been building a treehouse in the backyard since dawn?Apakah kami sudah membangun rumah pohon di halaman belakang sejak fajar?
They (+) They have been cleaning the old warehouse for hours.Mereka sudah membersihkan gudang tua itu selama berjam-jam.
(−) They have not been cleaning the old warehouse for hours.Mereka belum membersihkan gudang tua itu selama berjam-jam.
(?) Have they been cleaning the old warehouse for hours?Apakah mereka sudah membersihkan gudang tua itu selama berjam-jam?
HAVE BEEN  →  I, You, We, They
HAS BEEN  →  He, She, It
5
Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Positif — 've been / 's been
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I have been→I've been
you have been→you've been
he has been→he's been
she has been→she's been
it has been→it's been
we have been→we've been
they have been→they've been
Negatif — haven't been / hasn't been
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I have not been→I haven't been
you have not been→you haven't been
he has not been→he hasn't been
she has not been→she hasn't been
it has not been→it hasn't been
we have not been→we haven't been
they have not been→they haven't been
Contoh Kalimat
I've been jogging around the park every evening.Saya sudah berlari mengelilingi taman setiap sore. = I have been jogging around the park every evening.
You've been watching that crime series for hours.Kamu sudah menonton serial kriminal itu selama berjam-jam. = You have been watching that crime series for hours.
He's been fixing the leaking faucet since noon.Dia sudah memperbaiki keran yang bocor sejak siang. = He has been fixing the leaking faucet since noon.
She's been baking cookies all afternoon.Dia sudah memanggang kue kering sepanjang sore. = She has been baking cookies all afternoon.
It's been snowing steadily since midnight.Salju sudah turun terus-menerus sejak tengah malam. = It has been snowing steadily since midnight.
We've been planning the wedding for six months.Kami sudah merencanakan pernikahan itu selama enam bulan. = We have been planning the wedding for six months.
They've been arguing about politics lately.Mereka akhir-akhir ini sering berdebat tentang politik. = They have been arguing about politics lately.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The mechanics have been inspecting the engine since early morning.Para montir sudah memeriksa mesin itu sejak pagi-pagi sekali. Subject: the mechanics (jamak) → have been
The gardener has been trimming the hedges all day.Tukang kebun sudah memangkas semak pagar sepanjang hari. Subject: the gardener (tunggal) → has been
The orchestra has been rehearsing the symphony for weeks.Orkestra itu sudah berlatih simfoni selama berminggu-minggu. Subject: the orchestra (tunggal) → has been
The volunteers have been distributing warm meals since sunrise.Para relawan sudah membagikan makanan hangat sejak matahari terbit. Subject: the volunteers (jamak) → have been
The journalist has been investigating the corruption scandal for months.Wartawan itu sudah menyelidiki skandal korupsi selama berbulan-bulan. Subject: the journalist (tunggal) → has been
💡 Tips: Contraction 's been dan 've been sangat umum dalam percakapan sehari-hari. Hati-hati: he's di sini berarti he has, bukan "he is". Untuk negatif gunakan haven't been / hasn't been, dan jangan lupa akhiran -ing pada kata kerjanya.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
I have been knowing him since high school.
Salah: kata kerja stative seperti "know" tidak dipakai dalam bentuk continuous
✓
I have known him since high school.
Benar: gunakan Present Perfect biasa (have + V3) untuk verb stative seperti know, love, believe
✕
She has been work at the bakery all day.
Salah: kata kerja setelah "been" wajib memakai akhiran -ing
✓
She has been working at the bakery all day.
Benar: has been + working (V-ing)
✕
They have working on the project since Monday.
Salah: kata "been" tidak boleh dihilangkan — polanya selalu have/has + been + V-ing
✓
They have been working on the project since Monday.
Benar: have been + working — jangan lupa "been" di antara have/has dan V-ing
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Present Perfect Continuous dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
since
for
all day
all morning
how long
lately
recently
the whole week
Jika salah satu muncul + ada have/has been + V-ing → Present Perfect Continuous Tense
Since this morning, the technicians have been upgrading the network.
We have been hiking the mountain trail for six hours.
She has been organizing the charity gala lately.
How long have you been collecting vintage stamps?
The puppy has been chewing the slipper all day.
💡 Kata since (titik mula waktu) dan for (lama durasi) adalah penanda paling khas tense ini. Pertanyaan how long hampir selalu menuntut jawaban dalam Present Perfect Continuous.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola have/has + been + V-ing — jangan lupa "been" dan akhiran "-ing" pada kata kerjanya.
Pakai has been untuk he/she/it dan have been untuk I/you/we/they.
Tense ini menekankan aksi yang dimulai di masa lalu dan masih berlangsung atau baru saja berhenti dengan hasil yang terasa sekarang — sering bersama since dan for.
Kata kerja stative (know, love, believe, own) tidak dipakai dalam bentuk continuous — gunakan Present Perfect biasa.
