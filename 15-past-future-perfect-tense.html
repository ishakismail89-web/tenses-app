Past Future Perfect
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + would have + V3 (Past Participle)
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
Aksi yang akan sudah selesai sebelum titik masa depan — dilihat dari masa lalu
Menggambarkan tindakan yang (saat itu) diperkirakan akan sudah tuntas sebelum suatu titik waktu, dilihat dari sudut pandang masa lalu.
"She knew she would have repaired the engine by dawn."
Hasil pada conditional type 3
Menyatakan hasil pengandaian yang tidak terjadi: if + had + V3, ... would have + V3.
"If they had left earlier, they would have caught the train."
Reported speech dari Future Perfect
Saat kalimat "will have + V3" diceritakan kembali, "will" berubah menjadi "would".
"He said he would have painted the fence by Sunday."
Penyesalan / pengandaian tentang masa lalu
Mengungkapkan sesuatu yang seharusnya terjadi tetapi tidak, sebagai penyesalan atas masa lalu.
"I would have forgiven the mistake under different circumstances."
2
Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat
(+) Positif
S+would have+V3
(−) Negatif
S+wouldnothave+V3
(?) Tanya
Would+S+have+V3?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ would have = auxiliary verb (sama untuk semua subjek)  |  ■ V3 = past participle (bentuk ketiga kata kerja)
3
Contoh Kalimat
Penggunaan nyata dalam kalimat
She was certain that by midnight she would have memorized the entire speech.Dia yakin bahwa pada tengah malam dia sudah menghafal seluruh pidato itu. Aksi yang akan sudah selesai sebelum titik masa depan, dilihat dari masa lalu
If the rescuers had arrived sooner, they would have saved the stranded climbers.Seandainya para penyelamat tiba lebih cepat, mereka pasti sudah menyelamatkan para pendaki yang terjebak. Hasil pada conditional type 3 (if + had + V3)
The manager promised that the warehouse would have shipped every order by then.Manajer berjanji bahwa gudang itu sudah mengirimkan setiap pesanan pada saat itu. Reported speech dari Future Perfect (will have → would have)
With a little more patience, he would have understood her decision completely.Dengan sedikit lebih sabar, dia pasti sudah memahami keputusannya sepenuhnya. Penyesalan / pengandaian tentang masa lalu
Would the volunteers have planted all the trees by the next day?Apakah para relawan sudah menanam semua pohon pada keesokan harinya? Bentuk pertanyaan: Would + S + have + V3
4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ WOULD HAVE — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Aturan auxiliary: Past Future Perfect memakai would have untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan bentuk antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they. Kata kerja selalu dalam bentuk V3 (past participle).
(+)I would have repaired the leaking roof before the storm hit.Saya pasti sudah memperbaiki atap yang bocor sebelum badai datang.
(−)I would not have repaired the leaking roof before the storm hit.Saya tidak akan memperbaiki atap yang bocor sebelum badai datang.
(?)Would I have repaired the leaking roof before the storm hit?Apakah saya sudah memperbaiki atap yang bocor sebelum badai datang?
(+)He would have memorized the poem by the rehearsal.Dia pasti sudah menghafal puisi itu pada saat gladi resik.
(−)He would not have memorized the poem by the rehearsal.Dia tidak akan menghafal puisi itu pada saat gladi resik.
(?)Would he have memorized the poem by the rehearsal?Apakah dia sudah menghafal puisi itu pada saat gladi resik?
(+)She would have harvested the rice field before the flood.Dia pasti sudah memanen sawah itu sebelum banjir.
(−)She would not have harvested the rice field before the flood.Dia tidak akan memanen sawah itu sebelum banjir.
(?)Would she have harvested the rice field before the flood?Apakah dia sudah memanen sawah itu sebelum banjir?
(+)It would have charged the battery fully by morning.Alat itu pasti sudah mengisi baterai penuh pada pagi hari.
(−)It would not have charged the battery fully by morning.Alat itu tidak akan mengisi baterai penuh pada pagi hari.
(?)Would it have charged the battery fully by morning?Apakah alat itu sudah mengisi baterai penuh pada pagi hari?
(+)You would have translated the document before the client called.Kamu pasti sudah menerjemahkan dokumen itu sebelum klien menelepon.
(−)You would not have translated the document before the client called.Kamu tidak akan menerjemahkan dokumen itu sebelum klien menelepon.
(?)Would you have translated the document before the client called?Apakah kamu sudah menerjemahkan dokumen itu sebelum klien menelepon?
(+)We would have rehearsed the whole play by that time.Kami pasti sudah berlatih seluruh drama itu pada saat itu.
(−)We would not have rehearsed the whole play by that time.Kami tidak akan berlatih seluruh drama itu pada saat itu.
(?)Would we have rehearsed the whole play by that time?Apakah kami sudah berlatih seluruh drama itu pada saat itu?
(+)They would have restored the old temple before the festival.Mereka pasti sudah memugar candi tua itu sebelum festival.
(−)They would not have restored the old temple before the festival.Mereka tidak akan memugar candi tua itu sebelum festival.
(?)Would they have restored the old temple before the festival?Apakah mereka sudah memugar candi tua itu sebelum festival?
WOULD HAVE  →  I, He, She, It, You, We, They (semua subjek)
5
Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Positif — 'd have
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I would have→I'd have
you would have→you'd have
he would have→he'd have
she would have→she'd have
it would have→it'd have
we would have→we'd have
they would have→they'd have
Negatif — wouldn't have
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I would not have→I wouldn't have
you would not have→you wouldn't have
he would not have→he wouldn't have
she would not have→she wouldn't have
it would not have→it wouldn't have
we would not have→we wouldn't have
they would not have→they wouldn't have
Contoh Kalimat
With one extra hour, I'd have assembled the entire bookshelf myself.Dengan satu jam tambahan, saya pasti sudah merakit sendiri seluruh rak buku itu. = With one extra hour, I would have assembled the entire bookshelf myself.
If you had warned us, you'd have avoided the traffic jam easily.Seandainya kamu memperingatkan kami, kamu pasti sudah menghindari kemacetan itu dengan mudah. = If you had warned us, you would have avoided the traffic jam easily.
He told me that he'd have mailed the invitation by Friday.Dia memberi tahu saya bahwa dia sudah mengirimkan undangan itu pada hari Jumat. = He told me that he would have mailed the invitation by Friday.
Given more funding, she'd have expanded the orphanage that year.Dengan dana lebih, dia pasti sudah memperluas panti asuhan itu pada tahun tersebut. = Given more funding, she would have expanded the orphanage that year.
Honestly, I wouldn't have ignored such an obvious warning sign.Sejujurnya, saya tidak akan mengabaikan tanda peringatan yang begitu jelas. = Honestly, I would not have ignored such an obvious warning sign.
Without the storm, we'd have reached the summit before sunset.Tanpa badai itu, kami pasti sudah mencapai puncak sebelum matahari terbenam. = Without the storm, we would have reached the summit before sunset.
With a clearer map, they'd have located the hidden cave faster.Dengan peta yang lebih jelas, mereka pasti sudah menemukan gua tersembunyi itu lebih cepat. = With a clearer map, they would have located the hidden cave faster.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The orchestra would have tuned every instrument before the concert began.Orkestra itu pasti sudah menyetel setiap alat musik sebelum konser dimulai. Subject: the orchestra — bukan pronoun
The young apprentice would have forged the iron gate by spring.Si pemagang muda pasti sudah menempa gerbang besi itu pada musim semi. Subject: the young apprentice — bukan pronoun
The negotiation team would have settled the dispute peacefully that week.Tim negosiasi itu pasti sudah menyelesaikan perselisihan itu secara damai pada minggu tersebut. Subject: the negotiation team — bukan pronoun
The village baker would have baked a hundred loaves before dawn.Tukang roti desa itu pasti sudah memanggang seratus roti sebelum fajar. Subject: the village baker — bukan pronoun
The cleaning staff would have polished the marble floor by opening time.Petugas kebersihan itu pasti sudah memoles lantai marmer pada jam buka. Subject: the cleaning staff — bukan pronoun
💡 Tips: Contraction wouldn't have umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (would not have). Ingat: contraction 'd di sini berarti would dan selalu diikuti have sebelum V3.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
She would have went home before the rain started.
Salah: setelah "would have" wajib memakai V3 (past participle), bukan bentuk past simple (went)
✓
She would have gone home before the rain started.
Benar: would have + V3 (gone)
✕
They would of finished the marathon by noon.
Salah: bentuknya adalah "would HAVE", bukan "would of" — kesalahan ini muncul karena pelafalan yang mirip
✓
They would have finished the marathon by noon.
Benar: gunakan would have, bukan "would of"
✕
He would have repair the broken bridge by then.
Salah: setelah "would have" tidak boleh memakai base verb (repair) — harus V3
✓
He would have repaired the broken bridge by then.
Benar: would have + V3 (repaired)
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Future Perfect dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
by then
by that time
by the next day
before
if + had + V3
he said (that)
Jika muncul dalam konteks reported speech atau conditional type 3 + ada would have + V3 → Past Future Perfect Tense
By then, the firefighters would have extinguished the entire blaze.
By that time, the tailor would have stitched every costume.
The captain assured us the cargo would have departed by the next day.
If she had studied harder, she would have passed the bar exam before summer.
He claimed that the gardeners would have trimmed every hedge by then.
💡 Tense ini hampir selalu muncul dalam reported speech (will have → would have) atau sebagai hasil conditional type 3 (if + had + V3, ... would have + V3). Kata before dan by then menjadi penanda khas penyelesaian aksi dilihat dari masa lalu.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola would have + V3 — jangan lupa memakai bentuk ketiga (past participle), bukan past simple atau base verb.
Gunakan would have untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
Tense ini sering muncul pada reported speech (will have → would have) dan hasil conditional type 3 (if + had + V3).
Hindari kesalahan klasik "would of" — yang benar selalu would have. Contraction: 'd have dan wouldn't have.
