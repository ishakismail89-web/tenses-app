Past Perfect
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + had + V3 (Past Participle)
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
✅
Aksi selesai sebelum aksi lampau lainnya
Menggambarkan tindakan yang sudah tuntas sebelum tindakan lain di masa lampau terjadi.
"The train had departed before we reached the platform."
🔢
Menunjukkan urutan dua peristiwa lampau
Menegaskan peristiwa mana yang terjadi lebih dulu di antara dua kejadian masa lampau.
"After she had locked the gate, the alarm went off."
💬
Reported speech
Mengubah present perfect atau past perfect dalam kalimat tidak langsung menjadi past perfect.
"He said he had repaired the broken fence."
🔀
Third conditional
Mengandaikan situasi yang tidak terjadi di masa lampau dengan pola if + had + V3.
"If they had saved earlier, they would have bought the house."
2
Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat
(+) Positif
S+had+V3
(−) Negatif
S+hadnot+V3
(?) Tanya
Had+S+V3?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ had = auxiliary verb (sama untuk semua subjek)  |  ■ V3 = past participle (bentuk ketiga kata kerja)
3
Contoh Kalimat
Penggunaan nyata dalam kalimat
By the time the guests arrived, the chef had prepared a five-course dinner.Pada saat para tamu tiba, koki itu sudah menyiapkan makan malam lima hidangan. Aksi selesai sebelum aksi lampau lainnya terjadi
After the storm had passed, the villagers inspected their flooded fields.Setelah badai berlalu, para penduduk desa memeriksa ladang mereka yang terendam. Menunjukkan urutan: badai berlalu lebih dulu, lalu pemeriksaan
The detective realized that the suspect had hidden the stolen jewels in the attic.Detektif itu menyadari bahwa tersangka telah menyembunyikan permata curian di loteng. Aksi lebih dulu (menyembunyikan) sebelum kesadaran detektif
My grandmother told me that she had planted those mango trees decades ago.Nenek saya bercerita bahwa ia telah menanam pohon-pohon mangga itu beberapa dekade lalu. Reported speech — peristiwa yang lebih dulu di masa lampau
Had the pilot checked the fuel level before the flight took off?Apakah pilot itu sudah memeriksa kadar bahan bakar sebelum penerbangan lepas landas? Bentuk tanya — menanyakan aksi yang terjadi lebih dulu
4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ HAD — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Keunggulan Past Perfect: Auxiliary verb had digunakan untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan bentuk antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they. Selalu diikuti V3 (past participle).
PronounTipeKalimat

(−) I had not finished the crossword puzzle before breakfast was served.Saya belum menyelesaikan teka-teki silang sebelum sarapan dihidangkan.
(?) Had I finished the crossword puzzle before breakfast was served?Apakah saya sudah menyelesaikan teka-teki silang sebelum sarapan dihidangkan?

(?) Had he repaired the leaking roof before the heavy rain began?Apakah dia sudah memperbaiki atap yang bocor sebelum hujan deras turun?

Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Positif — 'd (= had)
Bentuk LengkapContraction

she had→she'd
it had→it'd
we had→we'd
they had→they'd
Negatif — hadn't
Bentuk LengkapContraction

you had not→you hadn't

Contoh Kalimat
By midnight, I'd wrapped every gift on the long shopping list.Pada tengah malam, saya sudah membungkus setiap hadiah dalam daftar belanja yang panjang. = By midnight, I had wrapped every gift on the long shopping list.
You'd mailed the application before the office closed for the holiday.Kamu sudah mengirim lamaran sebelum kantor tutup untuk libur. = You had mailed the application before the office closed for the holiday.
He'd sketched the whole mural before the paint even dried.Dia sudah membuat sketsa seluruh mural sebelum catnya bahkan mengering. = He had sketched the whole mural before the paint even dried.
She'd watered every flowerbed before the gardener noticed.Dia sudah menyirami setiap petak bunga sebelum tukang kebun menyadarinya. = She had watered every flowerbed before the gardener noticed.
We'd rehearsed the play twice before the director joined us.Kami sudah berlatih drama itu dua kali sebelum sutradara bergabung dengan kami. = We had rehearsed the play twice before the director joined us.
They'd sold the old motorcycle before the new model launched.Mereka sudah menjual sepeda motor lama sebelum model baru diluncurkan. = They had sold the old motorcycle before the new model launched.
The waiter realized he hadn't cleared the corner table all evening.Pelayan itu menyadari ia belum membereskan meja sudut sepanjang malam. = ... he had not cleared the corner table all evening.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The orchestra had tuned their instruments before the conductor raised his baton.Orkestra itu sudah menyetel alat musik mereka sebelum sang konduktor mengangkat tongkatnya. Subject: the orchestra — bukan pronoun
The librarian had shelved every returned book before the library reopened.Pustakawan itu sudah menata setiap buku yang dikembalikan sebelum perpustakaan dibuka kembali. Subject: the librarian — bukan pronoun
The volcano had erupted twice before the scientists installed the new sensors.Gunung berapi itu sudah meletus dua kali sebelum para ilmuwan memasang sensor baru. Subject: the volcano — bukan pronoun
The bakery had sold all its croissants before the morning rush ended.Toko roti itu sudah menjual habis semua croissant-nya sebelum keramaian pagi berakhir. Subject: the bakery — bukan pronoun
The firefighters had contained the blaze before reinforcements showed up.Para pemadam kebakaran sudah mengendalikan api sebelum bantuan tambahan datang. Subject: the firefighters — bukan pronoun
💡 Tips: Contraction 'd dapat berarti had (Past Perfect) atau would — bedakan dari konteks: jika diikuti V3 (past participle), maka 'd = had. Negatif hadn't umum dalam percakapan; untuk tulisan formal gunakan had not.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
She had wrote the letter before she sealed the envelope.
Salah: setelah "had" wajib memakai V3 (past participle), bukan bentuk lampau (wrote)
✓
She had written the letter before she sealed the envelope.
Benar: had + V3 (written)
✕
After I had ate dinner, I left the restaurant.
Salah: "ate" adalah V2 (bentuk lampau) — setelah "had" harus V3
✓
After I had eaten dinner, I left the restaurant.
Benar: had + V3 (eaten)
✕
They had not arrive when the ceremony started.
Salah: bentuk dasar (arrive) tidak boleh dipakai — negatif Past Perfect tetap butuh V3
✓
They had not arrived when the ceremony started.
Benar: had not + V3 (arrived)
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Perfect dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
before
after
by the time
already
when
until then
by then
no sooner ... than
Jika salah satu muncul + ada had + V3 → Past Perfect Tense
Before the curtain rose, the actors had warmed up backstage.
The carpenter had sanded the table after the glue had set.
By the time the ambulance came, the nurse had bandaged the wound.
The cashier had already counted the till when the manager walked in.
No sooner had the bell rung than the students had dashed outside.
💡 Frasa by the time, before, dan after adalah penanda khas tense ini. Ingat: Past Perfect selalu menunjuk aksi yang terjadi lebih dulu di antara dua peristiwa lampau.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola had + V3 — jangan lupa memakai bentuk ketiga (past participle) dari kata kerja setelah "had", bukan V1 atau V2.
Gunakan had untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
Past Perfect menandai aksi yang terjadi lebih dulu dari aksi lampau lainnya — sering dipasangkan dengan Simple Past.
Contraction positif 'd = had (cek dari V3 setelahnya); negatif hadn't. Penting juga untuk reported speech dan third conditional (if + had + V3).
