Past Future
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + would + V1 (Bentuk Dasar)
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
⏳
Masa depan dilihat dari sudut pandang masa lalu
Menyatakan sesuatu yang dianggap akan terjadi, dilihat dari titik waktu di masa lampau (future-in-the-past).
"In 2010, nobody knew that smartphones would dominate the market."
🗣️
Reported speech (mengubah "will" menjadi "would")
Saat melaporkan ucapan orang lain, "will" pada kalimat asli berubah menjadi "would".
"She said that she would bake a cake for the party."
🔀
Hasil pada conditional type 2
Menyatakan akibat yang tidak nyata pada kalimat pengandaian (if + past simple, ... would + V1).
"If I won the lottery, I would travel around the world."
🎯
Rencana atau niat masa lalu yang dipandang ke depan
Menggambarkan rencana atau niat yang dibuat di masa lalu untuk sebuah masa depan (dari sudut pandang waktu itu).
"They promised that they would repair the old bridge."

Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat

S+would+V1
(−) Negatif
S+wouldnot+V1

Would+S+V1?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ would = modal auxiliary (sama untuk semua subjek)  |  ■ V1 = bentuk dasar kata kerja (tanpa -s/-ed)
3
Contoh Kalimat
Penggunaan nyata dalam kalimat
The captain announced that the ship would depart the harbor at dawn.Kapten mengumumkan bahwa kapal akan berangkat dari pelabuhan saat fajar. Masa depan dilihat dari sudut pandang masa lalu (future-in-the-past)
My grandmother said that she would knit a warm sweater for the winter.Nenek saya berkata bahwa dia akan merajut sweter hangat untuk musim dingin. Reported speech — "will knit" berubah menjadi "would knit"
If the museum lowered its prices, more tourists would visit the exhibition.Jika museum menurunkan harganya, lebih banyak turis akan mengunjungi pameran itu. Hasil pada conditional type 2 (if + past, ... would + V1)
The mayor promised that the city would plant thousands of trees the next year.Wali kota berjanji bahwa kota itu akan menanam ribuan pohon tahun berikutnya. Rencana/niat masa lalu yang dipandang ke depan
Would the volunteers distribute the blankets later that day?Apakah para relawan akan membagikan selimut nanti pada hari itu? Bentuk pertanyaan — Would + S + V1?
4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ WOULD — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Aturan auxiliary Past Future: Modal would dipakai untuk semua subjek dan selalu diikuti V1 (bentuk dasar). Tidak ada perbedaan bentuk antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they. Alternatifnya adalah was/were going to + V1 (was untuk I/he/she/it, were untuk you/we/they).
PronounTipeKalimat
I (+) I knew that I would join the climbing expedition.Saya tahu bahwa saya akan ikut ekspedisi pendakian.
(−) I knew that I would not join the climbing expedition.Saya tahu bahwa saya tidak akan ikut ekspedisi pendakian.
(?) Would I join the climbing expedition?Apakah saya akan ikut ekspedisi pendakian?
He (+) He hoped that he would win the chess tournament.Dia berharap bahwa dia akan memenangkan turnamen catur.
(−) He hoped that he would not win the chess tournament.Dia berharap bahwa dia tidak akan memenangkan turnamen catur.
(?) Would he win the chess tournament?Apakah dia akan memenangkan turnamen catur?
She (+) She believed that she would paint a mural on the school wall.Dia percaya bahwa dia akan melukis mural di tembok sekolah.
(−) She believed that she would not paint a mural on the school wall.Dia percaya bahwa dia tidak akan melukis mural di tembok sekolah.
(?) Would she paint a mural on the school wall?Apakah dia akan melukis mural di tembok sekolah?
It (+) The forecast said that it would rain the following morning.Ramalan cuaca mengatakan bahwa hujan akan turun pada pagi berikutnya.
(−) The forecast said that it would not rain the following morning.Ramalan cuaca mengatakan bahwa hujan tidak akan turun pada pagi berikutnya.
(?) Would it rain the following morning?Apakah hujan akan turun pada pagi berikutnya?
You (+) You said that you would translate the ancient manuscript.Kamu berkata bahwa kamu akan menerjemahkan manuskrip kuno itu.
(−) You said that you would not translate the ancient manuscript.Kamu berkata bahwa kamu tidak akan menerjemahkan manuskrip kuno itu.
(?) Would you translate the ancient manuscript?Apakah kamu akan menerjemahkan manuskrip kuno itu?
We (+) We assumed that we would harvest the rice before the storm.Kami mengira bahwa kami akan memanen padi sebelum badai datang.
(−) We assumed that we would not harvest the rice before the storm.Kami mengira bahwa kami tidak akan memanen padi sebelum badai datang.
(?) Would we harvest the rice before the storm?Apakah kami akan memanen padi sebelum badai datang?
They (+) They expected that they would adopt a rescue puppy that weekend.Mereka berharap bahwa mereka akan mengadopsi anak anjing terlantar akhir pekan itu.
(−) They expected that they would not adopt a rescue puppy that weekend.Mereka berharap bahwa mereka tidak akan mengadopsi anak anjing terlantar akhir pekan itu.
(?) Would they adopt a rescue puppy that weekend?Apakah mereka akan mengadopsi anak anjing terlantar akhir pekan itu?
WOULD + V1  →  I, He, She, It, You, We, They (semua subjek)

Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Positif — 'd (= would)
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I would→I'd
you would→you'd
he would→he'd
she would→she'd
it would→it'd
we would→we'd
they would→they'd
Negatif — wouldn't (= would not)
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I would not→I wouldn't
you would not→you wouldn't
he would not→he wouldn't
she would not→she wouldn't
it would not→it wouldn't
we would not→we wouldn't
they would not→they wouldn't
Contoh Kalimat
He whispered that he'd fix the leaking roof that afternoon.Dia berbisik bahwa dia akan memperbaiki atap yang bocor sore itu. = He whispered that he would fix the leaking roof that afternoon.
I promised that I'd water the orchids while they were away.Saya berjanji bahwa saya akan menyiram anggrek selama mereka pergi. = I promised that I would water the orchids while they were away.
She mentioned that she'd record the podcast the next day.Dia menyebutkan bahwa dia akan merekam podcast keesokan harinya. = She mentioned that she would record the podcast the next day.
We agreed that we'd sponsor the local football team.Kami sepakat bahwa kami akan mensponsori tim sepak bola lokal. = We agreed that we would sponsor the local football team.
They guaranteed that they'd refund the broken laptop.Mereka menjamin bahwa mereka akan mengembalikan uang untuk laptop yang rusak. = They guaranteed that they would refund the broken laptop.
The coach insisted that the rookie wouldn't miss the final practice.Pelatih menegaskan bahwa pemain baru itu tidak akan melewatkan latihan terakhir. = ...that the rookie would not miss the final practice.
You assured me that you wouldn't reveal the secret recipe.Kamu meyakinkanku bahwa kamu tidak akan membocorkan resep rahasia itu. = ...that you would not reveal the secret recipe.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The director announced that the orchestra would perform a new symphony.Sutradara mengumumkan bahwa orkestra akan menampilkan simfoni baru. Subject: the orchestra — bukan pronoun
The pilot informed us that the airplane would land in twenty minutes.Pilot memberi tahu kami bahwa pesawat akan mendarat dalam dua puluh menit. Subject: the airplane — bukan pronoun
The teacher explained that the experiment would prove the hypothesis.Guru menjelaskan bahwa eksperimen itu akan membuktikan hipotesis tersebut. Subject: the experiment — bukan pronoun
The committee decided that the festival would feature local artists.Panitia memutuskan bahwa festival itu akan menampilkan seniman lokal. Subject: the festival — bukan pronoun
The scientist predicted that the volcano would erupt within a decade.Ilmuwan itu memprediksi bahwa gunung berapi itu akan meletus dalam satu dekade. Subject: the volcano — bukan pronoun
💡 Tips: Contraction wouldn't umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (would not). Ingat: contraction 'd di sini berarti would dan selalu diikuti V1.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
He said he would to help the neighbors move.
Salah: setelah "would" tidak boleh ada "to" — langsung pakai V1 (bentuk dasar)
✓
He said he would help the neighbors move.
Benar: would + V1 (help) tanpa "to"
✕
She would went to the seminar the next week.
Salah: setelah "would" wajib memakai V1, bukan bentuk lampau (went)
✓
She would go to the seminar the next week.
Benar: would + V1 (go)
✕
Setiap hari kecil dulu, ia "would swim" di sungai — lalu disangka itu Past Future.
Salah: di sini "would swim" hanya bermakna kebiasaan lampau, BUKAN fungsi future-in-the-past
✓
He said that he would swim across the lake the following day. → Past Future.
Benar: di Past Future, "would" menyatakan masa depan yang dilihat dari masa lalu (ada konteks reported speech / rencana), bukan sekadar kebiasaan lampau
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Future dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
He said that...
the next day
the following week
later that day
at that time
the next year
that weekend
if + past (type 2)
Jika ada konteks masa lalu (reported speech / pengandaian) + would + V1 → Past Future Tense
He said that the band would release a new album.
The guide assured us that the bus would arrive later that day.
The following week, the bakery would unveil its seasonal menu.
If she studied harder, she would pass the entrance exam.
We were certain that the train would leave the next day.
💡 Konteks reported speech ("He said that...") dan penanda waktu yang menengok ke depan dari masa lalu (the next day, the following week) adalah ciri khas tense ini. Pada kalimat pengandaian type 2 (if + past), klausa hasilnya juga memakai would + V1.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola would + V1 — kata kerja setelah "would" memakai bentuk dasar, tanpa "to", tanpa -s/-ed.
Gunakan would untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they. Alternatif: was/were going to + V1.
Past Future menyatakan masa depan yang dilihat dari masa lalu (future-in-the-past) — muncul di reported speech (will → would) dan hasil conditional type 2, BUKAN sekadar kebiasaan lampau.
Contraction wouldn't umum di percakapan — gunakan would not untuk tulisan formal. Contraction positif: 'd (= would).
