Past Future Perfect Continuous
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + would have been + V-ing
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
⏳
Durasi aksi hingga titik masa depan (dilihat dari masa lalu)
Menggambarkan aksi yang akan telah berlangsung selama suatu durasi hingga sebuah titik di masa depan, namun dipandang dari sudut masa lalu.
"By June, she would have been teaching for ten years."
🗣️
Reported speech dari future perfect continuous
Mengubah kalimat "will have been V-ing" menjadi bentuk lampau ketika dilaporkan kembali.
"He said they would have been driving for hours."
🔮
Pengandaian durasi yang berkelanjutan (conditional)
Menyatakan seberapa lama suatu proses akan telah berlangsung seandainya suatu syarat terpenuhi.
"By then, I would have been saving money for a year."
📐
Penekanan lamanya proses sebelum momen masa depan
Menonjolkan durasi sebuah aktivitas yang berkesinambungan sebelum momen tertentu, dari perspektif lampau.
"We would have been rehearsing for weeks before the show."

Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat

Bentuk LengkapContraction

Contoh Kalimat
She told me that by spring I'd have been volunteering at the shelter for a year.Dia memberitahuku bahwa pada musim semi saya sudah menjadi relawan di penampungan selama setahun. = I would have been volunteering at the shelter for a year.

We knew he'd have been repairing the engine for three days by the weekend.Kami tahu dia sudah memperbaiki mesin itu selama tiga hari pada akhir pekan. = he would have been repairing the engine for three days by the weekend.
I assumed she'd have been rehearsing the symphony for months before the tour.Saya mengira dia sudah berlatih simfoni itu selama berbulan-bulan sebelum tur. = she would have been rehearsing the symphony for months before the tour.
He doubted that we'd have been harvesting the wheat for long by the festival.Dia ragu bahwa kami sudah memanen gandum dalam waktu lama pada saat festival. = we would have been harvesting the wheat for long by the festival.
Without backup, they wouldn't have been guarding the border for weeks.Tanpa bantuan, mereka tidak akan menjaga perbatasan selama berminggu-minggu. = they would not have been guarding the border for weeks.
She admitted that it'd have been charging the batteries all night by sunrise.Dia mengakui bahwa alat itu sudah mengisi baterai sepanjang malam pada saat matahari terbit. = it would have been charging the batteries all night by sunrise.

💡 Tips: Contraction wouldn't have been umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (would not have been). Ingat: contraction 'd di sini berarti would, selalu diikuti have been sebelum V-ing.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
She would have being working at the clinic for years.
Salah: setelah "would have" memakai "been", bukan "being"
✓
She would have been working at the clinic for years.
Benar: would have been + V-ing (working)
✕
They would of been waiting for the train for hours.
Salah: yang benar adalah "would have", bukan "would of" (kesalahan dari bunyi yang mirip)
✓
They would have been waiting for the train for hours.
Benar: would have been + V-ing (waiting)
✕
He would have been work in the field since morning.
Salah: lupa menambahkan akhiran -ing pada kata kerja setelah "been"
✓
He would have been working in the field since morning.
Benar: would have been + V-ing (working)
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Future Perfect Continuous dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
for
by then
by that time
before
since
for hours
for years
all day
Jika penanda durasi muncul + ada would have been + V-ing, sering dalam konteks reported speech atau pengandaian → Past Future Perfect Continuous Tense
She said that by then the gardeners would have been pruning the orchard for weeks.
We believed the divers would have been exploring the reef for three hours.
He claimed the journalists would have been investigating the scandal before the verdict.
By that time, the apprentice would have been forging the blade since dawn.
If the funding had held, the scientists would have been testing the vaccine for a year.
💡 Penanda durasi seperti for dan since ditambah frasa by then / by that time adalah ciri khas tense ini. Karena bersifat lampau-masa-depan, kalimatnya hampir selalu muncul dalam reported speech atau pengandaian (conditional).

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola would have been + V-ing — gunakan been (bukan "being") dan jangan lupa akhiran -ing pada kata kerjanya.
Gunakan would have been untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
Tense ini menekankan durasi aksi yang akan telah berlangsung hingga titik masa depan, dipandang dari sudut masa lalu — sering muncul dalam reported speech dan pengandaian.
Contraction positif: 'd have been ('d = would); negatif: wouldn't have been di percakapan, would not have been untuk tulisan formal. Hindari "would of".
