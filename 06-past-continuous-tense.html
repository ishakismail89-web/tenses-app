Past Continuous
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + was/were + V-ing
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
⏳
Aksi sedang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu
Menggambarkan tindakan yang sedang terjadi pada titik waktu spesifik di masa lampau.
"At 8 PM yesterday, I was baking a chocolate cake."
⚡
Aksi terganggu oleh aksi lain
Aksi panjang yang sedang berlangsung dipotong oleh aksi pendek (when + simple past).
"She was reading a novel when the doorbell rang."
🔀
Dua aksi paralel di masa lalu
Dua kejadian yang berlangsung bersamaan, biasanya dihubungkan dengan "while".
"While he was cooking dinner, we were setting the table."
🎬
Latar belakang atau suasana dalam cerita
Menggambarkan situasi atau suasana yang sedang berlangsung sebagai latar sebuah kisah.
"The rain was falling softly over the quiet village."

Rumus Kalimat

Was/Were+S+V-ing?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ was/were = auxiliary verb (was untuk I/he/she/it, were untuk you/we/they)  |  ■ V-ing = kata kerja bentuk -ing (present participle)
3
Contoh Kalimat
Penggunaan nyata dalam kalimat
At 7 o'clock last night, I was repairing my old bicycle.Pada pukul 7 tadi malam, saya sedang memperbaiki sepeda tua saya. Aksi yang sedang berlangsung pada titik waktu tertentu di masa lalu
The children were painting a colorful mural when the teacher walked in.Anak-anak sedang melukis mural berwarna-warni ketika guru itu masuk. Aksi panjang yang terganggu oleh aksi pendek (when + simple past)
While my sister was practicing the violin, I was writing a letter.Sementara adik perempuan saya sedang berlatih biola, saya sedang menulis surat. Dua aksi paralel yang berlangsung bersamaan (while)
The wind was howling through the trees as the storm approached.Angin sedang menderu di antara pepohonan saat badai mendekat. Latar belakang atau suasana dalam sebuah cerita
Were the engineers testing the new bridge at noon yesterday?Apakah para insinyur sedang menguji jembatan baru pada siang hari kemarin? Pertanyaan tentang aksi yang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu
4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ WAS / WERE — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Aturan auxiliary Past Continuous: Gunakan was untuk subjek tunggal I, he, she, it dan were untuk subjek jamak you, we, they. Setelah was/were selalu diikuti kata kerja bentuk -ing.
PronounTipeKalimat
I (+) I was sketching a portrait in the park.Saya sedang menggambar sebuah potret di taman.
(−) I was not sketching a portrait in the park.Saya tidak sedang menggambar sebuah potret di taman.
(?) Was I sketching a portrait in the park?Apakah saya sedang menggambar sebuah potret di taman?
He (+) He was fixing the leaking faucet this morning.Dia sedang memperbaiki keran yang bocor tadi pagi.
(−) He was not fixing the leaking faucet this morning.Dia tidak sedang memperbaiki keran yang bocor tadi pagi.
(?) Was he fixing the leaking faucet this morning?Apakah dia sedang memperbaiki keran yang bocor tadi pagi?
She (+) She was watering the garden flowers at dawn.Dia sedang menyiram bunga-bunga taman saat fajar.
(−) She was not watering the garden flowers at dawn.Dia tidak sedang menyiram bunga-bunga taman saat fajar.
(?) Was she watering the garden flowers at dawn?Apakah dia sedang menyiram bunga-bunga taman saat fajar?
It (+) It was charging the battery when the power went out.Alat itu sedang mengisi baterai ketika listrik padam.
(−) It was not charging the battery when the power went out.Alat itu tidak sedang mengisi baterai ketika listrik padam.
(?) Was it charging the battery when the power went out?Apakah alat itu sedang mengisi baterai ketika listrik padam?
You (+) You were jogging along the beach at sunrise.Kamu sedang berlari santai di sepanjang pantai saat matahari terbit.
(−) You were not jogging along the beach at sunrise.Kamu tidak sedang berlari santai di sepanjang pantai saat matahari terbit.
(?) Were you jogging along the beach at sunrise?Apakah kamu sedang berlari santai di sepanjang pantai saat matahari terbit?
We (+) We were rehearsing a school play all afternoon.Kami sedang berlatih sebuah drama sekolah sepanjang sore.
(−) We were not rehearsing a school play all afternoon.Kami tidak sedang berlatih sebuah drama sekolah sepanjang sore.
(?) Were we rehearsing a school play all afternoon?Apakah kami sedang berlatih sebuah drama sekolah sepanjang sore?
They (+) They were harvesting the ripe coffee beans on the hill.Mereka sedang memanen biji kopi yang matang di bukit.
(−) They were not harvesting the ripe coffee beans on the hill.Mereka tidak sedang memanen biji kopi yang matang di bukit.
(?) Were they harvesting the ripe coffee beans on the hill?Apakah mereka sedang memanen biji kopi yang matang di bukit?
WAS  →  I, He, She, It
WERE  →  You, We, They
5
Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Negatif — wasn't / weren't
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I was not→I wasn't
he was not→he wasn't
she was not→she wasn't
it was not→it wasn't
you were not→you weren't
we were not→we weren't
they were not→they weren't
ℹ️ Catatan: Bentuk positif was dan were tidak memiliki contraction — yang disingkat hanya bentuk negatifnya menjadi wasn't dan weren't.
Contoh Kalimat
I wasn't listening to the radio when you called.Saya tidak sedang mendengarkan radio ketika kamu menelepon. = I was not listening to the radio when you called.
He wasn't driving the truck during the heavy storm.Dia tidak sedang mengemudikan truk selama badai hebat. = He was not driving the truck during the heavy storm.
She wasn't feeding the chickens at that moment.Dia tidak sedang memberi makan ayam-ayam pada saat itu. = She was not feeding the chickens at that moment.
The printer wasn't scanning the documents this afternoon.Mesin cetak itu tidak sedang memindai dokumen-dokumen siang tadi. = The printer was not scanning the documents this afternoon.
You weren't studying for the exam last night.Kamu tidak sedang belajar untuk ujian tadi malam. = You were not studying for the exam last night.
We weren't decorating the hall when the guests arrived.Kami tidak sedang menghias aula ketika para tamu tiba. = We were not decorating the hall when the guests arrived.
They weren't cleaning the stadium after the match.Mereka tidak sedang membersihkan stadion setelah pertandingan. = They were not cleaning the stadium after the match.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The orchestra was performing a symphony when the lights suddenly dimmed.Orkestra itu sedang membawakan sebuah simfoni ketika lampu tiba-tiba meredup. Subject: the orchestra (tunggal) → was
The firefighters were rescuing a trapped kitten from the rooftop.Para pemadam kebakaran sedang menyelamatkan seekor anak kucing yang terjebak dari atap. Subject: the firefighters (jamak) → were
The river was flowing gently beneath the wooden bridge.Sungai itu sedang mengalir dengan tenang di bawah jembatan kayu. Subject: the river (tunggal) → was
The volunteers were distributing warm blankets to the flood victims.Para relawan sedang membagikan selimut hangat kepada para korban banjir. Subject: the volunteers (jamak) → were
The old clock was ticking loudly in the silent library.Jam tua itu sedang berdetak nyaring di perpustakaan yang sunyi. Subject: the old clock (tunggal) → was
💡 Tips: Contraction wasn't dan weren't umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (was not / were not). Ingat: subjek tunggal pakai was, subjek jamak pakai were.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
I were watching a documentary at midnight.
Salah: subjek "I" harus memakai "was", bukan "were"
✓
I was watching a documentary at midnight.
Benar: I → was (subjek tunggal)
✕
She was play the piano in the living room.
Salah: setelah was/were kata kerja wajib memakai akhiran -ing, bukan bentuk dasar
✓
She was playing the piano in the living room.
Benar: was + playing (V-ing)
✕
They was running across the muddy field.
Salah: subjek jamak "they" harus memakai "were", bukan "was"
✓
They were running across the muddy field.
Benar: they → were (subjek jamak)
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Continuous dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
while
when
at 8 PM yesterday
at that moment
all evening
this time yesterday
as
Jika salah satu muncul + ada was/were + V-ing → Past Continuous Tense
While the chef was grilling the steak, the waiter was arranging the plates.
The audience was clapping when the curtain finally rose.
At 8 PM yesterday, we were assembling a wooden bookshelf.
This time yesterday, she was boarding a train to the mountains.
The tourists were taking photographs as the sun was setting.
💡 Kata while biasanya menandai aksi panjang yang sedang berlangsung, sedangkan when sering memperkenalkan aksi pendek (simple past) yang menginterupsi. Penanda waktu seperti at + jam + yesterday menunjukkan momen spesifik di masa lalu.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola was/were + V-ing — jangan lupa menambahkan akhiran -ing pada kata kerja setelah was/were.
Pilih auxiliary dengan benar: was untuk I, he, she, it dan were untuk you, we, they.
Past Continuous menekankan aksi yang sedang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu di masa lalu, sering dikombinasikan dengan while (aksi paralel) atau when (aksi yang menginterupsi).
Bentuk positif (was/were) tidak punya contraction, tetapi negatifnya bisa disingkat menjadi wasn't dan weren't.
