Past Future Continuous
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + would be + V-ing
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
Aksi yang akan sedang berlangsung — dilihat dari masa lalu
Menggambarkan tindakan yang akan sedang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu, dipandang dari sudut masa lalu.
"He knew she would be sleeping at midnight."
Reported speech dari Future Continuous
Mengubah kalimat "will be + V-ing" menjadi "would be + V-ing" saat menceritakan ucapan seseorang.
"She said she would be travelling to Rome."
Rencana berkelanjutan masa depan dari sudut masa lalu
Menyatakan rencana yang akan terus berlangsung, namun dilihat dari titik waktu di masa lampau.
"They promised they would be hosting the gala."
Dugaan tentang aksi yang sedang berlangsung
Menyatakan perkiraan bahwa suatu aktivitas kemungkinan sedang berlangsung pada momen tertentu.
"I assumed he would be rehearsing his speech."
2
Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat
(+) Positif
S+would be+V-ing
(−) Negatif
S+wouldnotbe+V-ing
(?) Tanya
Would+S+be+V-ing?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ would be = auxiliary verb (sama untuk semua subjek)  |  ■ V-ing = present participle (kata kerja + -ing)
3
Contoh Kalimat
Penggunaan nyata dalam kalimat
He told me that the children would be rehearsing the school play that afternoon.Dia memberitahuku bahwa anak-anak akan sedang melatih drama sekolah sore itu. Reported speech dari "will be rehearsing" (will be → would be)
The next morning, the volunteers would be planting hundreds of mangrove seedlings along the coast.Keesokan paginya, para relawan akan sedang menanam ratusan bibit bakau di sepanjang pantai. Aksi yang akan sedang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu, dilihat dari masa lalu
She assumed her brother would be repairing the old motorcycle at that time.Dia menduga kakaknya akan sedang memperbaiki sepeda motor tua pada saat itu. Dugaan tentang aksi yang sedang berlangsung pada momen tertentu
Later that evening, the chefs would be preparing a five-course dinner for the guests.Nanti malam itu, para koki akan sedang menyiapkan hidangan lima sajian untuk para tamu. Rencana berkelanjutan masa depan yang dipandang dari masa lalu
Would the engineers be testing the new bridge this time the following day?Apakah para insinyur akan sedang menguji jembatan baru pada waktu yang sama keesokan harinya? Kalimat tanya tentang aksi berkelanjutan di masa depan dari sudut masa lalu
4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ WOULD BE — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Keunggulan Past Future Continuous: Auxiliary would be digunakan untuk semua subjek + V-ing — tidak ada perbedaan bentuk antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
(+)I would be painting the garden fence the next morning.Saya akan sedang mengecat pagar taman keesokan paginya.
(−)I would not be painting the garden fence the next morning.Saya tidak akan sedang mengecat pagar taman keesokan paginya.
(?)Would I be painting the garden fence the next morning?Apakah saya akan sedang mengecat pagar taman keesokan paginya?
(+)He would be delivering the morning newspapers at that time.Dia akan sedang mengantarkan koran pagi pada saat itu.
(−)He would not be delivering the morning newspapers at that time.Dia tidak akan sedang mengantarkan koran pagi pada saat itu.
(?)Would he be delivering the morning newspapers at that time?Apakah dia akan sedang mengantarkan koran pagi pada saat itu?
(+)She would be conducting the orchestra later that evening.Dia akan sedang memimpin orkestra nanti malam itu.
(−)She would not be conducting the orchestra later that evening.Dia tidak akan sedang memimpin orkestra nanti malam itu.
(?)Would she be conducting the orchestra later that evening?Apakah dia akan sedang memimpin orkestra nanti malam itu?
(+)It would be charging the batteries this time the following day.Alat itu akan sedang mengisi daya baterai pada waktu yang sama keesokan harinya.
(−)It would not be charging the batteries this time the following day.Alat itu tidak akan sedang mengisi daya baterai pada waktu yang sama keesokan harinya.
(?)Would it be charging the batteries this time the following day?Apakah alat itu akan sedang mengisi daya baterai pada waktu yang sama keesokan harinya?
(+)You would be guiding the tourists through the museum that afternoon.Kamu akan sedang memandu para wisatawan melewati museum sore itu.
(−)You would not be guiding the tourists through the museum that afternoon.Kamu tidak akan sedang memandu para wisatawan melewati museum sore itu.
(?)Would you be guiding the tourists through the museum that afternoon?Apakah kamu akan sedang memandu para wisatawan melewati museum sore itu?

(−)We would not be filming the documentary in the rainforest by then.Kami tidak akan sedang membuat film dokumenter di hutan hujan pada saat itu.

(?)Would they be harvesting the rice fields the following week?Apakah mereka akan sedang memanen sawah pada minggu berikutnya?
WOULD BE  →  I, He, She, It, You, We, They (semua subjek) + V-ing
5
Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Positif — 'd be
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I would be→I'd be
you would be→you'd be
he would be→he'd be
she would be→she'd be
it would be→it'd be
we would be→we'd be
they would be→they'd be
Negatif — wouldn't be
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I would not be→I wouldn't be
you would not be→you wouldn't be
he would not be→he wouldn't be
she would not be→she wouldn't be
it would not be→it wouldn't be
we would not be→we wouldn't be
they would not be→they wouldn't be
Contoh Kalimat
She said I'd be presenting the quarterly figures the next morning.Dia berkata bahwa saya akan sedang mempresentasikan angka kuartalan keesokan paginya. = She said I would be presenting the quarterly figures the next morning.
He promised you'd be leading the rescue mission at that time.Dia berjanji bahwa kamu akan sedang memimpin misi penyelamatan pada saat itu. = He promised you would be leading the rescue mission at that time.
We thought he'd be coaching the junior team later that evening.Kami mengira dia akan sedang melatih tim junior nanti malam itu. = We thought he would be coaching the junior team later that evening.
They believed she'd be negotiating the merger this time the following day.Mereka percaya dia akan sedang menegosiasikan merger pada waktu yang sama keesokan harinya. = They believed she would be negotiating the merger this time the following day.
I knew we'd be exploring the ancient ruins by then.Saya tahu kami akan sedang menjelajahi reruntuhan kuno pada saat itu. = I knew we would be exploring the ancient ruins by then.
The captain announced they'd be docking the vessel before dawn.Sang kapten mengumumkan bahwa mereka akan sedang menambatkan kapal sebelum fajar. = The captain announced they would be docking the vessel before dawn.
She warned that the machine wouldn't be running smoothly during the upgrade.Dia memperingatkan bahwa mesin itu tidak akan sedang berjalan lancar selama pembaruan. = ...the machine would not be running smoothly during the upgrade.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The night guard knew the security system would be scanning the corridors after midnight.Penjaga malam tahu sistem keamanan akan sedang memindai koridor setelah tengah malam. Subject: the security system — bukan pronoun
The director said the film crew would be shooting the final scene at dawn.Sutradara berkata bahwa kru film akan sedang merekam adegan terakhir saat fajar. Subject: the film crew — bukan pronoun
The teacher mentioned the students would be debating climate policy the following week.Guru menyebutkan bahwa para murid akan sedang memperdebatkan kebijakan iklim minggu berikutnya. Subject: the students — bukan pronoun
My grandmother believed the bakery would be selling fresh pastries by sunrise.Nenekku percaya toko roti itu akan sedang menjual kue-kue segar saat matahari terbit. Subject: the bakery — bukan pronoun
The coach expected the swimmers would be training in the pool early that day.Pelatih memperkirakan para perenang akan sedang berlatih di kolam pagi-pagi hari itu. Subject: the swimmers — bukan pronoun
💡 Tips: Contraction wouldn't be umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (would not be). Ingat: contraction 'd selalu diikuti be sebelum V-ing.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
He thought he would be go to the airport that night.
Salah: setelah "would be" wajib memakai V-ing, bukan bentuk dasar (go)
✓
He thought he would be going to the airport that night.
Benar: would be + V-ing (going)
✕
She would being cooking dinner for the guests.
Salah: bentuknya adalah "be", bukan "being" — polanya selalu would be + V-ing
✓
She would be cooking dinner for the guests.
Benar: would be + V-ing (cooking)
✕
They would be cook the meal the next morning.
Salah: lupa menambahkan akhiran -ing pada kata kerja setelah "would be"
✓
They would be cooking the meal the next morning.
Benar: would be + V-ing (cooking) — jangan lupa -ing
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Future Continuous dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
reported speech
the next morning
at that time
later that evening
this time the following day
by then
the following week
Jika muncul dalam konteks masa lalu + ada would be + V-ing → Past Future Continuous Tense
He said that, the next morning, the gardeners would be trimming the hedges.
At that time, the astronauts would be orbiting the planet.
She knew the band would be performing on stage later that evening.
This time the following day, the marathon runners would be crossing the finish line.
They expected the carpenters would be assembling the furniture by then.
💡 Tense ini paling sering muncul dalam reported speech — saat "will be + V-ing" berubah menjadi would be + V-ing. Penanda waktu seperti the next morning dan at that time menandakan sudut pandang masa lalu menuju masa depan.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola would be + V-ing — jangan lupa menambahkan akhiran -ing pada kata kerja setelah "be".
Gunakan would be untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
Past Future Continuous adalah versi reported speech dari Future Continuous: will be → would be, dipandang dari sudut masa lalu.
Contraction wouldn't be umum di percakapan — gunakan would not be untuk tulisan formal. Contraction positif: 'd be.
