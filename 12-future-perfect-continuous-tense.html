Future Perfect
Continuous Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + will have been + V-ing
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
⏳
Durasi aksi hingga titik tertentu di masa depan
Menggambarkan aksi yang terus berlangsung sampai sebuah titik waktu tertentu di masa depan.
"By noon, the passengers will have been waiting for three hours."
📈
Penekanan pada lamanya proses
Menyoroti betapa panjang sebuah proses berjalan sebelum momen tertentu di masa depan tiba.
"By midnight, she will have been studying for eight hours straight."
🌧️
Penyebab situasi di masa depan
Menjelaskan penyebab dari sebuah kondisi yang akan terlihat pada masa depan.
"The streets will be flooded because it will have been raining all week."
📅
Berapa lama aksi telah berjalan
Menyatakan berapa lama suatu aksi akan telah berlangsung pada satu waktu di masa depan.
"By June, he will have been teaching at this school for twenty years."
2
Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat
(+) Positif
S+will have been+V-ing
(−) Negatif
S+willnothave been+V-ing
(?) Tanya
Will+S+have been+V-ing?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ will have been = auxiliary verb (sama untuk semua subjek)  |  ■ V-ing = present participle (kata kerja + -ing)

4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ WILL HAVE BEEN — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Aturan Future Perfect Continuous: Auxiliary verb will have been digunakan untuk semua subjek + V-ing — tidak ada perbedaan bentuk antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.

5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The volunteers will have been distributing meals for six hours by sunset.Para relawan sudah membagikan makanan selama enam jam pada saat matahari terbenam. Subject: the volunteers — bukan pronoun
The new manager will have been leading the department for a year by March.Manajer baru itu sudah memimpin departemen selama satu tahun pada bulan Maret. Subject: the new manager — bukan pronoun
The orchestra will have been performing the symphony for two hours by intermission.Orkestra itu sudah membawakan simfoni selama dua jam pada saat jeda. Subject: the orchestra — bukan pronoun
The scientists will have been observing the comet for three nights by Friday.Para ilmuwan sudah mengamati komet itu selama tiga malam pada hari Jumat. Subject: the scientists — bukan pronoun
The bakery will have been selling fresh croissants for a decade by next spring.Toko roti itu sudah menjual croissant segar selama satu dekade pada musim semi mendatang. Subject: the bakery — bukan pronoun
💡 Tips: Contraction won't have been umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (will not have been). Ingat: contraction 'll selalu diikuti have been sebelum V-ing.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
By noon, the crew will have being working on the bridge for hours.
Salah: setelah "will have" harus "been", bukan "being" — polanya will have been + V-ing
✓
By noon, the crew will have been working on the bridge for hours.
Benar: will have been + working
✕
By midnight, the nurses will have been work the night shift for ten hours.
Salah: kata kerja setelah "been" wajib memakai akhiran -ing (V-ing), bukan bentuk dasar
✓
By midnight, the nurses will have been working the night shift for ten hours.
Benar: will have been + working
✕
By June, she will have been knowing the secret for a year.
Salah: stative verb seperti "know" tidak dipakai dalam bentuk continuous — gunakan Future Perfect biasa
✓
By June, she will have known the secret for a year.
Benar: dengan stative verb pakai Future Perfect (will have + V3), bukan continuous
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Future Perfect Continuous dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
by
for
by the time
by then
by next year
for ... by
Jika ada penanda durasi (for) yang dihitung sampai titik masa depan (by / by the time) + ada will have been + V-ing → Future Perfect Continuous Tense
By tomorrow morning, the firefighters will have been battling the blaze for twelve hours.
This time next year, my grandmother will have been knitting sweaters for charity for two decades.
The chef will have been marinating the lamb for six hours by dinnertime.
By the time the semester ends, I will have been tutoring students for four months.
The crew will have been filming the documentary for a year by the wrap party.
💡 Kombinasi for + durasi bersama by / by the time adalah penanda khas tense ini — fokusnya pada berapa lama aksi telah berjalan sampai titik masa depan, bukan sekadar selesai atau tidaknya.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola will have been + V-ing — jangan tertukar: setelah "have" pakai been (bukan being) dan kata kerjanya berakhiran -ing.
Gunakan will have been untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
Tense ini menekankan durasi sebuah aksi yang telah berlangsung sampai satu titik di masa depan — sering dipasangkan dengan for + lama waktu dan by / by the time.
Hindari stative verb (know, believe, own) dalam bentuk ini — gunakan Future Perfect biasa. Contraction: 'll have been dan won't have been.
