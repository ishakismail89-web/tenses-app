Past Perfect Continuous
Tense

Pelajari kapan, bagaimana, dan mengapa tense ini digunakan — lengkap dengan rumus, sinyal waktu, contraction, dan kesalahan yang harus dihindari.

S + had been + V-ing
1
Kegunaan
Kapan tense ini dipakai?
Durasi aksi sebelum titik waktu lampau
Menggambarkan aksi yang telah berlangsung selama suatu periode sebelum momen tertentu di masa lalu.
"She had been studying for six hours before the exam."
Penyebab situasi lampau (efeknya terlihat)
Menjelaskan alasan dari sebuah kondisi di masa lalu yang efeknya masih terlihat saat itu.
"His shoes were muddy because he had been hiking all morning."
Menekankan lamanya aksi sebelum aksi lain
Menonjolkan berapa lama suatu aksi berjalan sebelum aksi lampau lain memutusnya.
"They had been waiting for an hour when the bus finally arrived."
Reported speech dari present perfect continuous
Mengubah kalimat present perfect continuous menjadi bentuk lampau saat melaporkan ucapan.
"He said he had been painting the fence all day."
2
Rumus Kalimat
Pola untuk semua jenis kalimat
(+) Positif
S+had been+V-ing
(−) Negatif
S+hadnotbeen+V-ing
(?) Tanya
Had+S+been+V-ing?
■ S = Subjek  |  ■ had been = auxiliary verb (sama untuk semua subjek)  |  ■ V-ing = present participle (kata kerja + akhiran -ing)

4
Positif · Negatif · Tanya
Semua pronoun — dari I sampai They
✅ HAD BEEN — Semua Subjek (I · He · She · It · You · We · They)
💡 Keunggulan Past Perfect Continuous: Auxiliary verb had been digunakan untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan bentuk antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.

(−)I had not been editing the documentary for hours before the deadline.Saya belum menyunting film dokumenter itu selama berjam-jam sebelum tenggat waktu.
(?)Had I been editing the documentary for hours before the deadline?Apakah saya telah menyunting film dokumenter itu selama berjam-jam sebelum tenggat waktu?

(?)Had he been jogging along the river since sunrise?Apakah dia telah berlari santai di tepi sungai sejak matahari terbit?

5
Contraction (Bentuk Singkat)
Versi singkat yang sering dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari
Positif — 'd been
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I had been→I'd been
you had been→you'd been
he had been→he'd been
she had been→she'd been
it had been→it'd been
we had been→we'd been
they had been→they'd been
Negatif — hadn't been
Bentuk LengkapContraction
I had not been→I hadn't been
you had not been→you hadn't been
he had not been→he hadn't been
she had not been→she hadn't been
it had not been→it hadn't been
we had not been→we hadn't been
they had not been→they hadn't been
Contoh Kalimat
By the time we met, I'd been travelling across Asia for a year.Saat kita bertemu, aku telah berkeliling Asia selama setahun. = By the time we met, I had been travelling across Asia for a year.
You'd been complaining about the noise long before the neighbours apologized.Kamu telah mengeluhkan kebisingan itu jauh sebelum para tetangga meminta maaf. = You had been complaining about the noise long before the neighbours apologized.
He'd been coaching the team for a decade before he retired.Dia telah melatih tim itu selama satu dekade sebelum pensiun. = He had been coaching the team for a decade before he retired.
She'd been saving money quietly until she bought her first apartment.Dia telah menabung diam-diam sampai akhirnya membeli apartemen pertamanya. = She had been saving money quietly until she bought her first apartment.
We'd been rehearsing the choir piece before the conductor changed the key.Kami telah berlatih lagu paduan suara itu sebelum sang dirigen mengubah nada dasarnya. = We had been rehearsing the choir piece before the conductor changed the key.
They'd been arguing about the budget until the manager stepped in.Mereka telah berdebat soal anggaran sampai sang manajer turun tangan. = They had been arguing about the budget until the manager stepped in.
The patient hadn't been sleeping well for weeks before the diagnosis.Pasien itu belum tidur nyenyak selama berminggu-minggu sebelum diagnosis. = The patient had not been sleeping well for weeks before the diagnosis.
5 Contoh dengan Subject Lain (Bukan Pronoun)
The factory workers had been protesting outside the gate for three days before management responded.Para buruh pabrik telah berunjuk rasa di luar gerbang selama tiga hari sebelum manajemen menanggapi. Subject: the factory workers — bukan pronoun
The old printer had been jamming repeatedly before the technician replaced it.Printer tua itu telah macet berulang kali sebelum teknisi menggantinya. Subject: the old printer — bukan pronoun
The marketing department had been testing the campaign for weeks before the launch.Departemen pemasaran telah menguji kampanye itu selama berminggu-minggu sebelum peluncuran. Subject: the marketing department — bukan pronoun
The stray dog had been wandering the alley for hours before someone fed it.Anjing liar itu telah berkeliaran di gang selama berjam-jam sebelum seseorang memberinya makan. Subject: the stray dog — bukan pronoun
The fire alarm had been ringing for minutes before anyone evacuated the building.Alarm kebakaran itu telah berbunyi selama beberapa menit sebelum ada yang mengosongkan gedung. Subject: the fire alarm — bukan pronoun
💡 Tips: Contraction hadn't been umum dipakai dalam percakapan sehari-hari dan tulisan informal. Untuk tulisan formal, gunakan bentuk lengkap (had not been). Ingat: contraction 'd di sini berarti had dan selalu diikuti been sebelum V-ing.
6
Kesalahan Umum
Error yang sering terjadi dan cara menghindarinya
✕
She had been work hard all semester.
Salah: setelah "had been" wajib memakai V-ing (present participle), bukan bentuk dasar (work)
✓
She had been working hard all semester.
Benar: had been + V-ing (working)
✕
They had working all night on the project.
Salah: kata "been" tidak boleh dihilangkan — polanya selalu had + been + V-ing
✓
They had been working all night on the project.
Benar: had been + V-ing (working)
✕
I had been knowing the answer the whole time.
Salah: kata kerja stative seperti "know" tidak dipakai dalam bentuk continuous
✓
I had known the answer the whole time.
Benar: gunakan Past Perfect biasa (had + V3) untuk stative verb
7
Time Signal & Cara Cepat Mengenali
Penanda waktu + deteksi Past Perfect Continuous dalam satu langkah
🔍 Cari time signal-nya terlebih dulu
for
since
before
how long
until then
by the time
all day
Jika salah satu muncul + ada had been + V-ing → Past Perfect Continuous Tense
The athletes had been training for months before the championship.
She had been managing the café since her father fell ill.
We had been cleaning the attic all day when the guests suddenly arrived.
By the time the rescuers came, the climbers had been shivering for hours.
He had been lecturing until then, so the students looked exhausted.
💡 Frasa for dan since menekankan durasi, sedangkan before dan by the time menandai aksi lampau lain yang menjadi titik acuan. Kombinasi penanda durasi + titik waktu lampau adalah ciri khas tense ini.

👉 Kunci Utama

Selalu gunakan pola had been + V-ing — jangan lupa akhiran -ing pada kata kerja dan jangan menghilangkan kata "been".
Gunakan had been untuk semua subjek — tidak ada perbedaan antara I, he, she, it, you, we, they.
Tense ini menekankan durasi sebuah aksi yang berlangsung sebelum titik atau aksi tertentu di masa lalu, dan sering menjelaskan penyebabnya.
Hindari kata kerja stative (know, believe, own) dalam bentuk ini — pakai Past Perfect biasa. Contraction: 'd been (positif) dan hadn't been (negatif).
